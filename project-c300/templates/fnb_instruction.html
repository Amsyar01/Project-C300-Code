Therapist Instructions For Assessment

Welcome to F&B vocational training assessment.
Please listen the instructions carefully.
Today, you are required to perform the following 4 tasks in this assessment
1. Cook a bowl of maggi mee soup
2. Fry onion omelette
3. Make a cup of milo (milo:20g and sugar:2g)
4. Wash and dry a big pot
Now, I will show you the setup of the kitchen environment, including the location of all needed tools and materials and the operation of any needed electrical appliances where necessary.

(Orientation) Orientate client to each kitchen cabinet (from left to right cabinet in the sequences as follow) and fridge

a) (T01- plate/bowl/cup) Please use the green color cups/bowls/plates (for Muslim clients) or red colored cups/bowls/plates (for non-Muslim clients) for the assessment today.
b) (B02- cutlery eg fork, spoon) This is the cutlery eg fork and spoon cabinet.
c) (B03- Spatula, soup ladle etc) This is the spatula, soup ladle cabinet.
d) (B04- knife) Please use the “blue-color knife” for the assessment later (pointing to blue-color knife).
e) (B06- kettle, chopping board) This is kettle, chopping board cabinet.
f) (B08- big pot, frying pan) This is big pot and frying pan cabinet.
g) (Induction cooker- main switch (labelled as red) near to oven and 2nd switch is inside B09 cabinet)
(Demonstration) Now, I will show you how to operate the induction cooker.

OT to take note: Must show where to switch on/off the main switch of induction cooker, power on/off the induction cooker, how to adjust the power mode with +/- (min 400 watt and max 2200 watt). Do you have any questions?

h) (T04- weighing machine/onion/oil/maggi mee/milo/sugar) This is maggi mee, onion, oil, milo, sugar, weighing machine cabinet.
(Demonstration) Now I will show you how to operate the weighing machine.

OT to take note: Must show power on/off, tare mode with an item as demonstration. Do you have any questions?

i) (Fridge- egg) This is the egg.
j) (Cloth-red and blue/green on table) For the red colored cloth is to dry the tools/equipment after wash and blue colored cloth is to wipe the table.
Do you have any questions?
You are required to complete the assigned tasks and wash all the tools/items after use within 30 mins. If you do not meet the 30mins timing, you can continue to complete the tasks up to 60 mins. Once you have done with all the tasks including washing tasks, please let me know so that I will stop the timer.
(Standard time: 45 minutes)
You are required to focus on the tasks and aim to complete it to the best of your abilities.
You are required to re-close the opened containers, return the items to the original place after use, clean the table, wash and dry the used tools and discard any waste into a rubbish bin.
Please ensure you wash all the tools/equipment before you use it
You can refer to the instructions list provided to you.
Please do not hesitate to clarify any doubt or ask questions when need arises during the assessment period.
Do you have any questions? If no, you can read the instructions list first and do let me know when you are ready to start.
(Timer) Start the timer once client is ready.
