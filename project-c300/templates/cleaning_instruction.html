Therapist Manual For OcTAVE Cleaning Vocational Training Program Intake Assessment

Assessment venue: Common space in front of group therapy room and staircases lead to job club office
Required tools/materials: OT is required to set up the environment
Tools & Set Up
1. Water pail
2. Cloth
3. Broom
4. Dustpan
5. Mop
6. Trolley mop bucket
7. Caution wet floor warning signboard
8. 2 yellow chairs (take from Pottery workshop and place beside the coffee table)
9. Simulated rubbish on the floor (3 spots) and staircases (1 spot)

Therapist Instructions For Assessment

Welcome to cleaning vocational training assessment.
Please listen the instructions carefully.
Today, you are required to perform the following 4 tasks in this assessment
1. Wipe the coffee table, chairs and windows
2. Sweep the staircases
3. Sweep and mop the floor
4. Wipe the yellow chairs
Now, I will show you the setup of the cleaning store room and the areas to be cleaned, including the location of all needed tools and materials and the operation of any needed electrical appliances where necessary.

(Orientation 1) Orientate client to cleaning store room, level 1

This is the cleaning store room (pointing to the signboard at the door). You can find and use all the cleaning equipment/tools over here. Do you have any questions?

(Orientation 2) Orientate client to the common space in front of group therapy room, Level 1

(Bring client to that area when giving instructions to them). You are required to wipe the coffee table, chairs and windows over here.
For the chairs wiping, you just need to wipe the back rest, surface of the seat and arm rest. For yellow chairs, please return to the Pottery workshop after you have finished wiping it. Also, you are required to sweep and mop the floor from one end to another end. You have to sweep the floor first before mopping the floor. Make sure no water dripping from the mop. For the window wiping, you are required to wipe windows without opening it. Do you have any questions?
(OT to take note that if client asks where to pour the water of the trolley mop bucket after assessment- direct client to pour the water at toiletâ€™s squatting cubicle instead of cleaning store room)

(Orientation 3) Orientate client to the staircases lead to job club office.

You are required to sweep this staircase. Do you have any questions?
You are required to complete the assigned tasks within 30 mins. If you do not meet the 30mins timing, you can continue to complete the tasks up to 60 mins. Once you have done with all the tasks, please let me know so that I will stop the timer.
(Standard time: 45 minutes)
You are required to focus on the tasks and aim to complete it to the best of your abilities.
You are required return the items to the original place after use and discard any waste into a rubbish bin.
You can refer to the instructions list provided to you.
Please do not hesitate to clarify any doubt or ask questions when need arises during the assessment period.
Do you have any questions? If no, you can read the instructions list first and do let me know when you are ready to start.
(Timer) Start the timer once client is ready.
