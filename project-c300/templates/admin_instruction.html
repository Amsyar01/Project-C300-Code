Therapist Instructions For Assessment

Welcome to receptionist vocational training assessment.
Please listen the instructions carefully.
Today, you are required to perform the following 4 tasks in this assessment
1. Excel spreadsheet
2. Phone answering and taking phone messages
3. Presenting phone messages in Microsoft Word in one email format
4. Retrieving and sorting out files in a box
Now, I will show you the setup of job club office environment, including the location of all needed tools and materials and the operations of any needed electrical appliances where necessary.
(Orientation): Orientate client to job club office, level 1. Please use the computer no 3 for the assessment today. This is phone. This is stationary box etc. This is short ladder. This is the metal cabinet with blue colored files.
(Orientation): Orientate client to the small room inside job club office, level 1. This is the box.
TASK 1
Please see Excel spreadsheet table. You are required to complete an exact replica of the spreadsheet given. Please change the month/year to current and fill up the amount reimbursed column as well as calculate the Sub total.
Please SAVE this file as OTPaymentdatemonth (eg. OTPayment1905) in desktop.
Do you have any questions?
TASK 2 (OTs to note that all phone messages to be completed during the excel task ONLY)
Whilst you are performing excel tasks, you are expected to answer any calls and take down the messages accurately.
You are expected to show good phone etiquette skills and accuracy in message taking. Please address the caller in a polite manner. Do not allow caller to wait on phone for lengthy period whilst you take down the message.
You are expected to answer the call with this standardized opener:
“Good morning/afternoon OT recep (name) speaking. How can I assist you?" You are also expected to end the call with “Thank you have a nice day”.
When taking down the message, take down the following information ONLY:
1. Name of caller
2. Recipient of message
3. Content of message
You would be required to initiate a call as a follow up for one of the phone calls that you will receive. Please call 63892678 and deliver your message for this follow up call accurately.
Do you have any questions?
TASK 3
You are required to present all phone messages in ONE organized, email format in MS Word addressing to Mr Paul.
Please SAVE this file as Phonemessagesdatemonth (eg. Phonemessages1905) in desktop.
TASK 4
Please retrieve all blue files from all the compartment of metal cabinet and arrange the files alphabetically in the box provided to you. You will need to also take the box from the top of a cupboard. Once you have sorted out the files, place the box of files next to your computer.
Do you have any questions?
You are required to complete the assigned tasks within 45 mins. You can still go on to complete the tasks till 75 mins if you do not meet the 45 mins mark. Once you have done with all tasks, please let me know so that I will stop the timer. (Standard time: 60 minutes)
You are required to focus on the tasks at all times and aim to complete it to the best of your abilities.
You are required to return all items to their original place after use. If you need any items/materials, please approach the receptionist for assistance.
You can refer to the instructions list provided to you.
Please do not hesitate to clarify any doubt or ask questions when need arises during the assessment period.
Do you have any questions? If no, you can read the instructions list first and do let me know when you are ready to start.
(Timer) Start the timer once client is ready.
